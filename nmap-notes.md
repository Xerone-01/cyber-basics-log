# Nmap Basics 🧢

## What is Nmap?
Nmap (Network Mapper) is a tool used to scan networks and discover devices, open ports, and services running on a system.

It is widely used in cybersecurity and ethical hacking.

## What can it do?
- Find devices connected to a network
- Detect open ports
- Identify services (like SSH, HTTP)
- Help understand network structure

## Basic Commands:

- Scan your own device:
  nmap localhost

- Scan a local IP:
  nmap 192.168.1.1

- Scan a range of IPs:
  nmap 192.168.1.1-10

## Important Note:
Only use Nmap on systems you own or have permission to test.
